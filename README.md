# Lane Changers

A rough-draft mini-game designed to emulate the emotion of 'stress.' The player tries to quickly navigate through speeding lanes on the highway before they miss their exit.

---

## Gameplay Overview

The player must navigate from the far left lane to the right lane before they miss their exit! Dodge speeding cars while also following the law (using blinkers before changing lanes)! 


---

## Controls

| Action | Input |
|------|------|
| Speedup | W |
| Slowdow | S |
| Change Lanes | A/D |
| Blinker | Left/Right Click |
